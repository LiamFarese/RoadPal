RoadPal

Introduction

We have created a Weather app website.

Our brand new app called Road Pal will clearly be your best pal on the road. We have created this app in order to keep the transport go smoothly and the app best features are that you can keep it up to date by simply uploading news on it regarding the weather. This way everyone would know what the weather is like in all regions and areas. This way we can prevent many accidents and keep everyone aware on the road.

Mission Statement:

Our goal is to start small by helping smaller cities such as Cape york to help better prepare themselves for bad road condtions in their journeys. Our solution is to make a mobile app that can show accurate local weather data detailing zones ofinaccessibility and reports of disruption to vehicle access.

What Our App Can Do:

Report Features Such as:

  • Floods.
  • Fires.
  • Tornado.
  • Road Blocks.
  • Traffic.

Heat Maps and Data

HeatMaps. using live data taken from OpenLayers weather API. we can accurately display local weather information in local areas. This allows us to show you where the most affected areas are and makes a clear visual representation of areas you should avoid.

User Reports. A large part of our app is based off trust and user reports. If a user encounters an error they should report it on our app using a simple process. This is stored on our own database and processed and shown to other users in the area who might then encounter the same error.

Storing Previous Maps. In adverse weather condtions its likely that the internet may be out and connections to the internet may not be possible however using our service we keep a backup of previous maps meaing there will always be a solution with our app.
